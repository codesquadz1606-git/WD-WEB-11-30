Topic 2: What is positions?
Positions are of 5 types:
i. Static (Default position)
    In this position we cannot move the box.
ii. Absolute
    In absolute it moves from his parent position & leaves his space.
iii. Relative
    In relative it moves from his own position.
iv. Fixed 
    In this it fix at one position and leaves his space.
v. Sticky
    It will also fix at one place but will stick to his position.
